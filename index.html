<< The K.K. Slider Cover Project

Animal Crossing, a beloved game series from Nintendo, has been a largely influential game for me since I was first introduced to the series while studying design in university. The music, composed by Kazumi Totaka, is beloved by fans, especially that attributed to the charming guitar-plucking dog, K.K. Slider. Every week, the player can view a live performance of an original K.K. song, then take home a CD to play in their own in-game home.

As an avid fan of the Animal Crossing franchise and its music, I found the one disappointing aspect of K.K. Slider was the lack of lyrics to his 90-odd original songs, performed every week in the game. Thus, the concept for the K.K. Slider Cover Project was born in 2014. After meeting Rekcahdam in 2015, he agreed to collaborate with me by producing all-new instrumental tracks for my original lyrics.

At its height, Rekcahdam and I were writing, recording, and producing one song per week. Over the course of 2015-2016, we released 19 completed songs with accompanying YouTube videos. The album was released in January of 2016, when Rekcahdam and I performed "K.K. Cruisin'" as part of our set at MAGFest 2016. The album is available on Spotify, YouTube, BandCamp, iTunes and more.

Composer: Kazumi Totaka

Lyrics: Jay Andromeda
